The Legacy of Earth Sciences

Prithvi is the annual symposium of the Department of Geology and Geophysics along with the IIT Kharagpur student chapters of the Earth Science Study Circle (ESSC). The stage is set for a perfect meeting of academia, alumni, and industry.

Born in 2009, Prithvi has completed 16 golden years of creating an unmatched aura of a science and technology spectacle. It is one of the largest fests of Earth Sciences in India with participation from over 600 students from more than 60 universities.

Institute of National Importance

Being the first IIT to be established, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur has emerged as one of the most reputed technological institutions in the country.

  • Recognized by Government of India in 1956
  • Spans over 2100 acres of lush green campus
  • Hosts the largest number of departments and students
  • State-of-the-art research facilities across diverse fields
  • Home to the oldest and most renowned Geology Dept.

Major Events

Rock Riddle

Solo Challenge

An Earth science-themed crossword competition. Solve clues, beat the clock, and showcase your skills.

Geodyssey

Team of 3-4

Navigate through geoscience-themed clues and puzzles. Crack codes to uncover the final treasure.

Terra-Files

Team of 2-4

Analyze real-world scenarios in this case study competition and develop innovative geological solutions.

Ring of Fire

Team of 3

Head-to-head debates on thought-provoking Earth science topics. Challenge ideas and outshine opponents.

Minrex

Team of 1-3

Identify minerals, rocks, and fossils with precision. The definitive test of geological field skills.

Triviasaurus

Team of 3-4

Take on exciting rounds of challenging questions. Think fast and battle for the top spot.
